Born on the Beach

Aruba, 2024

"It started with a problem everyone knows: the Playlist Dictator."

The idea for Social Jukebox was born on a beach in Aruba, surrounded by friends and family. We watched one person struggle to keep the music going while trying to please everyone. Every time a song changed, someone else would roll their eyes or ask for the aux cord.

That's when it clicked: music at a gathering should be a conversation, not a monologue. So we built a system where the crowd wins—something that works as perfectly at a seaside bonfire as it does during a long road trip or an office jam session.

Family-tested, beach-approved, and ready to end the era of the Playlist Dictator.
Welcome to the Democratic Dancefloor.
