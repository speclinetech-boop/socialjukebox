Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Our Privacy Pledge

At Specline Tech LLC, we believe your music taste is your business. Social Jukebox is designed to be Privacy-First. We do not create user profiles, we do not track your location, and we do not sell your data. Period.

Data Collection

What we DON'T collect:

  • Names or Emails
  • Phone Numbers
  • Precise GPS Location
  • Contacts or Photos

What happens locally:

  • Apple Music authentication
  • Device-to-device voting
  • Temporary session IDs

Third Party Services

Social Jukebox utilizes the Apple Music API to facilitate music playback. Your interaction with music content is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not transmit your music library data to any external servers owned by Specline Tech LLC.
