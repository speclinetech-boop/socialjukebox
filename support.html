Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a party?
Tap "I'm the DJ" on the home screen. You'll get a 4-digit PIN to share with your guests. Add songs using the search feature and tap "Play Next Song" to start the music. Note: Hosting requires DJ Pro ($4.99 one-time purchase).
How do I join a party?
Tap "I'm a Guest" and enter the 4-digit PIN from the DJ. Make sure you're on the same WiFi network as the DJ. Once connected, you can add songs, vote, and send emoji reactions!
Do I need Apple Music?
Yes, both DJs and guests need an active Apple Music subscription to search for songs and play music. Social Jukebox uses Apple Music's full catalog.
What are Super Votes?
Super Votes give a song +5 votes instantly, pushing it toward the top of the queue. Everyone gets 3 free Super Votes per session. Use them on songs you really want to hear!
How do I clear the queue?
There's no way to remove individual songs. To clear the queue and start fresh, the DJ should end the current session and start a new one. Alternatively, you can downvote songs you don't want to hear—they'll move to the bottom.
Why can't I connect to a party?
Make sure you're on the same WiFi network as the DJ and entering the correct 4-digit PIN. If it still doesn't work, ask the DJ to restart their session. Sometimes it takes a few seconds to connect.
